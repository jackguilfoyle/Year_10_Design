Shin Splint routine

Primarly done before a hill workout after the warm up. Shin Splints are a stress fracture caused in you shin when you calf is not strong enough or if your over running. This routine can help lower you chance of getting a shin splint. Here is the routine being deomnstrated in a Video.

  1. 25 toe taps slow right foot
  2. 25 toe taps slow left foot
  3. 2 foot Bunny hop 20m
  4. Standing on tip toes, arms to the sky, toes pointed out and walk like this for 20m
  5. Standing on tip toes, arms to the sky, toes pointed in and walk like this for 20m
  6. Raise your toes and walk on heels for 20m
  7. 25 toe taps fast right foot
  8. 25 toe taps fast left foot
