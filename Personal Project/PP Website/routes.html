North York

Sunnybrook and Wilket Creek Loop
Distance: 6.5km
Terrain: Mainly asphalt, however graval through Glendon Forest
Elevation Change: Gradual uphill if compleated counter-clockwise with a steep downhill or if compleated clockwise a steep uphill with a gradual downhill.
This loop brings runners out of the city and into one of the city's largest parks on one of the nicest ravines. Runners can begin anywhere along the loop, however I suggest they start in the parking lot beside the dog park accessed through Sunnybrook Hospital.

Old Toronto

Yellow Creek and Moore Park Ravine Loop
Distance: 7.5km
Terrain: Mainly gravel or dirt. The red section segnifies a back country trail with more cross country style terrain/
Elevation Change: Gradual but signifiant elevation change with the Brickworks being much lower than Mt. Pleasent Cemetary.
This loop provides runners with the feeling of ravine running where the paths are more trails and theirs less asphalt. It also is a great longer run with hills loop as it provides a great long uphill no matter which way you run it.
